Justin’s Autumn Fruit Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chicken
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 3 Tablespoons oregano
  • dash Salt
  • dash black pepper
  • 1 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup pitted prunes
  • 1 cup dried apricots
  • 1 cup green olives
  • 1/2 cup capers
  • 8 bay leaves
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 4 Tablespoons fresh parsley

Directions

Combine everything except for the brown sugar, wine and parsley in a baking dish. Marinate overnight or for a few hours. Preheat oven to 350F. Sprinkle brown sugar over chicken and pour wine around it. Bake for 1 1/4 hours until golden brown, basting often. Serve with fresh parsley on top and with serve pan juices.
