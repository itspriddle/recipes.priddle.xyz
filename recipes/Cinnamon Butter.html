Cinnamon Butter

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¼ cup liquid honey
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt

Directions

Mix all ingredients together until smooth. Add additional salt, honey, or cinnamon to taste. Cover and refrigerate over night.
