6 Layer Taco Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 1 can (16oz) refried beans
  • 1 8oz pack cream cheese, softened
  • 1 container (16oz) sour cream
  • 1 jar (16oz) salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1 can (16oz) sliced black olives

Directions

In a medium bowl, mix taco seasoning and refried beans. Spread mixture onto a large serving dish. In another bowl, mix sour cream and cream cheese. Spread mix on top of beans. Top layers with salsa, cheddar cheese, iceberg lettuce and olives.
