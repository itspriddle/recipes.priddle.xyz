Roasted Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups cooked chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • ⅛ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp oregano

Directions

Preheat oven to 400°. Place all ingredients in one large bowl and mix until chickpeas are all coated. Place in baking tray. Cook for 15 minutes.
