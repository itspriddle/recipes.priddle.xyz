Milk Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup melted butter

Directions

Preheat oven to 355° F.

Mix milk, 1 tbsp sugar, yeast, bloom for 10 mins. Combine combine flour, salt, and remaining sugar in a bowl. Add 1 egg, butter, yeast mix. Knead for 5-10 minutes until dough is formed. Place in oiled bowl, cover, and proof for 60 minutes. Punch gas from dough. Roll flat to about ¼ inch thickness. Roll dough on itself to form loaves. Place in greased loaf pan. Proof for 30 minutes. Mix 1 egg and 1 tbsp milk, brush on top of loaf. Bake 18-20 minutes. Cool before slicing.
